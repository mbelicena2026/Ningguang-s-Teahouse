A teahouse, or tearoom, is an establishment which primarily serves tea and other light refreshments. A tea room may be a room set aside in a hotel especially for serving afternoon tea, or may be an establishment which only serves cream teas. In our teahouse, we wish to spark creativity within or locals by giving them a taste of home.
